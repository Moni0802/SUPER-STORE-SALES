## INSIGHTS AND SUGGESTIONS:
INSIGHTS 

1)  In OCT-2019 sales and Profit both are high, it may be due festival season but in OCT-2020  it was low and it can be due to pandemic situation.
2)  Maximum sales is done from the west region with maximum profit across all region that is 68k.
3)  Average no of days to delivery any product is 4.
4)  Maximum purchase occur in cash on delivery which shows customers are not confident in online shopping.
5)  Most of the purchasing has been done through consumer segment.
6)  Most sales has been done from Office Supplies.
7)  Most Sales has been done through Phones.
8)  Majority of people like standard shipment mode.
9)  Forecasting has also done which shows how will be the sales for  next 15 days based on old sales report.
10) California is in Top in terms of sales figures.


SUGGESTIONS

1)  Different offers or vouchers can be offer to other region customers in order to increase the sales specially in south region.
2)  Average no of days to deliver can be reduce.
3)  Should give special offers if customer does shopping cards or online.
4)  Special offers to choose first class delivery or second class delivery like a small cashback can be offer and its amount can be included in product.
